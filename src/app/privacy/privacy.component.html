Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{lastUpdated}}

Overview

MyDailyFace is a personal photo diary app that helps you capture and organize daily selfies. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

Data Collection

MyDailyFace does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data processing happens locally on your device.

Local Storage

  • Photos are stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB
  • App settings are stored in your browser's local storage
  • No personal information is collected or stored by us

Google Drive Integration (Optional)

If you choose to enable Google Drive backup:

  • We access your Google account only to create a dedicated "MyDailyFace" folder
  • Photos are uploaded directly from your device to your Google Drive
  • We do not access, read, or store any other files in your Google Drive
  • We do not store your Google credentials or tokens
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your photos remain:

  • On your local device
  • In your personal Google Drive (if you enable backup)
  • Under your complete control

Camera Access

The app requests camera access only to capture photos. Photos are processed locally and never sent to external servers without your explicit consent (Google Drive backup).

Data Security

  • All data remains on your device or in your personal Google Drive
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all external communications
  • Google Drive access uses OAuth 2.0 authentication

Data Retention

You control all data retention:

  • Delete photos anytime from the app
  • Clear all app data through browser settings
  • Remove Google Drive backup by deleting the MyDailyFace folder

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be updated on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
